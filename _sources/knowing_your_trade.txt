Data Science
============

    Get a sense of the industry - this blog gives an overview of the most read
    R-Post for jobs in 2014.

Newsletters
-----------
`Data Science Weekly <www.datascienceweekly.org>`_
    A free weekly newsletter featuring curated news, articles and jobs
    related to Data Science.

`Data Tau <www.datatau.com>`_
    A simple & streamlined newsletter with links to external sources.

High Quality Data Science Coursework
------------------------------------
`Bitbootcamp Resources <http://www.bitbootcamp.com/resources.html>`_
    Helpful list of Resources for Machine Learning\, Statistics and
    Computer Science.
`Clare Corthell\’s Open Source Data Science Masters <http://datasciencemasters.org/>`_
    The open-source curriculum for learning Data Science. Foundational in both
    theory and technologies, the OSDSM breaks down the core competencies
    necessary to make data useful.
`Andrew Ng\’s Coursera Machine Learning Class <https://www.coursera.org/course/ml>`_
    A broad introduction to machine learning, datamining, and statistical
    pattern recognition.
`Jeff Leek\’s Coursera Data Science Class Exploratory Data Analysis Class <https://www.coursera.org/course/exdata>`_
    Essential exploratory techniques for summarizing data.
`Chris Fonnesbeck\’s Advanced Statistical Computing Course <https://github.com/fonnesbeck/Bios366>`_
    Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC), Metropolis-Hastings, Gibbs sampling,
    estimation-maximization (EM) algorithms, data augmentation algorithms with
    applications for model fitting and techniques for dealing with missing
    data.

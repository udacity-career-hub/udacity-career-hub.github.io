*************************
Learn About Your Industry
*************************

Researching your industry is an important first step in the job process. Understanding your goal career in the broader context will make you much more efficient when applying to jobs, and give you a better idea of the skills that you might want to continue developing.

Links below are organized by specific industry, to help you jumpstart your research. For general (not specifically attached to any technical topic) information on how to get started researching your industry, check out our Career Development Workshop below.

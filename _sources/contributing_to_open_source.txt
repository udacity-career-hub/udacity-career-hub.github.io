.. _getting_started:

Getting Started
===============
* `Getting Started with Open Source Projects <http://blog.udacity.com/2013/10/get-started-with-open-source-projects.html>`_
    Written by Udacity’s own engineer and open source contributor, this
    article gives instructions on how to start contributing to open source
    projects.
* `Open Source Getting Started <http://blog.newrelic.com/2014/05/05/open-source_gettingstarted>`_
    This article does a great job of giving your ideas on how to contribute
    to open source projects in little and big ways - it all helps!
* `Contribute to Jquery <http://contribute.jquery.org/open-source/>`_
    Another great guide on open source - how, why,  and when to join the
    community are all covered here.
* `10 Ways to Get Started <http://opensource.com/life/13/1/10-ways-get-started-open-source>`_
    Don’t know where to get started with Open Source? This article outlines
    10 helpful ways to begin.

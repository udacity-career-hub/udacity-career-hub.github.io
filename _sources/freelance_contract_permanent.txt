.. _job_boards:

Job Boards
==========
`Remote OK <https://remoteok.io/>`_
    Not technically freelance, but focused on remote work.
`Elance <www.elance.com>`_
    Elance is awesome tool for finding freelance jobs especially for people
    in different countries since it handles the payment system.
`We Work Remotely <www.weworkremotely.com>`_
    A great place to find and list jobs that aren’t restricted by commutes
    or a particular geographic area.
`Truelancer <http://www.truelancer.com>`_
    Truelancer is a Global Community of Trusted Freelancers. Post your
    projects, find work and get hired.
`Top Coder <http://www.topcoder.com/>`_
    Technically a competition website, but at its core a place for freelance
    work.
`Guide to Freelance Contracts <http://skillcrush.com/2014/09/23/guide-to-freelance-contracts-legal/>`_
    A helpful guide to contracts working as a freelance worker.
`Send Five Emails <http://skillcrush.com/2014/11/21/send-5-emails/?hvid=3cUJtt>`_
    This article details five emails to send to get your freelance career
    off the  ground.

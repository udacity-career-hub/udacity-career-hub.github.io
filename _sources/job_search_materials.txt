General
=======
`10 Things Job Seekers Must Do to Get a Better Job <https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/20130911212503-15454-10-things-job-seekers-must-do-to-get-a-better-job>`_
    An informative article that features ten things to keep in mind when
    aplyting to jobs and interviewing.
`Career Dean <https://www.careerdean.com/>`_
    Search engine where you ask questions and get answers from software
    engineers.
`The Muse Job Search <https://www.themuse.com/tags/job-search>`_
    This website provides access to an array of articles related to
    job-searching best practices.
`Becoming an Apprentice <http://hugomelo.com/becoming-an-apprentice/>`_
    Interesting article gives personal advice on how to achieve success as a
    professional coding by being a Coding Apprentice. Great tips for career
    transitions or non-college grads.
`Jobpedia <http://www.jobipedia.org/>`_
    Jobpedia is a place for you to ask questions and search for information
    related to job searching and the interviewing process.

Resume
======
`Wellesley Resume Guide <http://www.wellesley.edu/sites/default/files/assets/departments/cws/files/resume_e-guide_2012_0.pdf>`_
    A Resume Guide E-Book from Wellesley Univeristy - not coding specific,
    but helpful information.
`Quick Changes to Get Your Resume Noticed <https://www.themuse.com/advice/45-quick-changes-that-help-your-resume-get-noticed-2>`_
    This article details 45 quick changes that will make your resume stand
    out.
`4 Things You Didn\'t Know You Could Put on Your Resume <https://www.themuse.com/advice/4-things-you-didnt-know-you-could-put-on-your-resume>`_
    Feeling unsure of what to put on your resume? This article is woth a read
    because it outlines 4 unexpected things you can put on your resume.
`Complete List of Action Verbs <http://www.wellesley.edu/sites/default/files/assets/departments/cws/files/complete_list_of_action_verbs.pdf>`_
    Check out this list of action verbs! It great a great resource when you
    are crafting your resume.

.. _cover_letters:

Cover Letters
=============
`Forbes <http://www.forbes.com/sites/dailymuse/2014/02/06/the-cover-letters-that-make-hiring-managers-smile-then-call-you/>`_
    Forbes’ guide to writing a concise and memorable cover letter.
`Job Search Cover Letter Samples <http://jobsearch.about.com/od/coverlettersamples/a/coverlettsample.htm>`_
    This resource provides several examples of cover letters.
`Resume Genius Cover Letter Guide <https://resumegenius.com/resume/cover-letters-the-how-to-guide>`_
    This article features the “Five Rules for Cover Letters” and provides
    sample letters.
`Cover Letters for Tech Jobs <http://mashable.com/2011/02/05/cover-letter-tech-jobs/>`_
    This article talks about the question - are cover letters relevant for
    today’s technology jobs? In arguing yes, it also provides helpful tips
    to make your cover letter relevant and pertinent to today’s context.

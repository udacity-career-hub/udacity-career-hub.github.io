*************************************
Resume
*************************************

Looking to brush up your resume? Here are several helpful links. While these are resources that pertain to non-technical resumes, these are still useful to provide some ways that you can highlight your accomplishments.

Check out Udacity's Career Development Workshop on how to refine your resume.

`Resume Resource from Wellesley College <http://www.wellesley.edu/sites/default/files/assets/departments/cws/files/resume_e-guide_2012_0.pdf>`_
    A Resume Guide E-Book from Wellesley College. 

`The Muse: 45 changes you can make that will help your resume stand out! <https://www.themuse.com/advice/45-quick-changes-that-help-your-resume-get-noticed-2>`_

`The Muse: 4 Unexpected Ways to Make Your Resume Stand Out <https://www.themuse.com/advice/4-things-you-didnt-know-you-could-put-on-your-resume>`_
    Feeling unsure of what to put on your resume? This article is worth a read because it outlines 4 unexpected things you can add that will make your resume stand out! 

`Change Your Action Verbs! <http://www.wellesley.edu/sites/default/files/assets/departments/cws/files/complete_list_of_action_verbs.pdf>`_ 
    Action verbs are important in strengthening your resume because it shows employers that you played a meaningful role in your previous work, and that you will continue to play such a role in a future opportunity. Check out this article for some ideas to diversify your verb choices.

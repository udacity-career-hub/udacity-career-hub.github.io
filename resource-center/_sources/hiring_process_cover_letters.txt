*************************************
Cover Letters
*************************************

A cover letter is typically your first opportunity to tell a recruiter or potential employer about yourself. Ensuring that your cover letter is memorable and impressive will ensure that you will be called back for phone & in-person interviews!


`The Cover Letters that Impress Hiring Managers <http://www.forbes.com/sites/dailymuse/2014/02/06/the-cover-letters-that-make-hiring-managers-smile-then-call-you/>`_
    Forbes’ guide to writing a concise and memorable cover letter.
 
`Cover Letter Examples <http://jobsearch.about.com/od/coverlettersamples/a/coverlettsample.htm>`_
    Still feeling stuck about how to write a cover letter? Check out these examples.

`Five Rules for Cover Letters <https://resumegenius.com/resume/cover-letters-the-how-to-guide>`_
    In addition to tips, this article also provides examples of cover letters. 

`Are Cover Letters Relevant for Today's Tech Jobs? <http://mashable.com/2011/02/05/cover-letter-tech-jobs/>`_
    In arguing yes, this article also provides helpful tips to make your cover letter relevant and pertinent to today’s context.

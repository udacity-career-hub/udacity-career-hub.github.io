.. _hiring_process_resume:

*************************************
Resume
*************************************

Looking to brush up your resume? Check out Udacity's `Career Development Workshop <https://docs.google.com/document/d/1fFKvktW0O1sHOfjg7pBHrgWg7t3BgRQkek4X-466aCk/pub?embedded=true>`_ on how to refine your resume. The video of the presentation can be found `here <https://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&v=vW8baYJBofg>`_.

Additionally, check out the following links that, although not focused on technical resumes, will still provide you with a few techniques on how best to highlight your technical accomplishments.

`Resume Resource from Wellesley College <http://www.wellesley.edu/sites/default/files/assets/departments/cws/files/resume_e-guide_2012_0.pdf>`_
    A Resume Guide E-Book from Wellesley College.

`The Muse <https://www.themuse.com/advice/45-quick-changes-that-help-your-resume-get-noticed-2>`_
	45 changes you can make that will help your resume stand out.

`Jobscan <https://www.jobscan.co/>`_
	Many companies filter job applications by putting them through automatic resume scanners. Check to see if your resume will make it pass the screen!

`The Muse: 4 Unexpected Ways to Make Your Resume Stand Out <https://www.themuse.com/advice/4-things-you-didnt-know-you-could-put-on-your-resume>`_
    Feeling unsure of what to put on your resume? This article is worth a read because it outlines 4 unexpected things you can add that will make your resume stand out!

`Change Your Action Verbs! <http://www.wellesley.edu/sites/default/files/assets/departments/cws/files/complete_list_of_action_verbs.pdf>`_
    Using action verbs consistently throughout your resume helps emphasize to employers your individual contributions and the meaningful role that you played in previous positions. Check out this list of action verbs to use in your resume.

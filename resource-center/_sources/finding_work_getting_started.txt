*************************************
Getting Started 
*************************************

Check out Udacity's `Career Development Workshop <https://docs.google.com/document/d/1XJf0wYWIoHWfM8Ep6xIkuBw6tmAlyMW5l37bEgeSoSA/pub?embedded=true>`_ which covers the basics on how to get started finding work opportunities. You can find the video to the workshop `here <https://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&v=OP1CJbQpMHA>`_. 

Additionally, here are two articles which give more specific advice on what to do and not do to land your next gig.
 
- `How to Get Your Next Interview <https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/20140924220547-15454-4-new-ideas-for-getting-your-next-job-interview>`_

- `Make Your Job Hunt More Efficient <https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/20140725005005-15454-why-posting-your-resume-is-the-least-effective-way-to-get-a-job>`_ 

If you've taken time off from work, for whatever reason, check out the following article on `How to explain gaps in your work history <http://advice.careerbuilder.com/posts/how-to-explain-gaps-in-your-work-history>`_.

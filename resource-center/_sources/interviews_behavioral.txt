==================
Phone Screening
==================

Phone interviews are often the first stage of the hiring process – doing well will increase your odds of being called back for an on-site interview! Check out these tips for succeeding in this section.

`Phone interview DOs and DON’Ts <http://www.yale.edu/hronline/startcareer/resources/docs/interview_tips.pdf>`_
    This is a great resource from Yale University for some initial tips for the phone interview.

`Tips from Gayle Laakman McDowell <http://news.dice.com/2012/10/02/gayle-laakmann-mcdowell-coding-interviews/>`_
    5 Steps to cracking your coding interview, from phone interview to onsite visit.

`Expert Advice: 8 Tips for Acing a Phone Interview <http://www.nerdwallet.com/blog/nerdscholar/2014/expert-advice-8-tips-acing-phone-interview/>`_
    8 quick tips from professionals about phone interviews.


`Palantir: How to Rock a Phone Interview <https://www.palantir.com/2012/09/how-to-rock-a-phone-interview/>`_
    More tips on how to ace your phone interview.

.. _contract_permanent:

=======================
Contract vs. Permanent
=======================

There are many different types of work that you could involve yourself in. Figure out which one is best for you using these articles below!

Trying to figure out your ideal work type? This `article <http://blog.udacity.com/2015/03/contract-vs-freelance-vs-full-time-can-tell-difference.html>`_ details the difference between freelance, contract, and full time work. 

Here’s `another article <http://blog.chegg.com/2014/02/07/how-to-be-an-independent-contractor-reporting-income-and-paying-taxes/>`_ on the financial logistics (such as how to pay your taxes) of being an independent contractor. 

Trying to decide whether to be an employee or an independent contractor? Check out these articles.

`"Should I Be An Employee Or An Independent Contractor?" <http://www.forbes.com/sites/steveparrish/2013/12/16/should-i-be-an-employee-or-an-independent-contractor/>`_
    This article discusses the pros and cons of independent contractor work compared to full-time employment.

`Important Differences You Need to Know <https://www.legalzoom.com/articles/employee-vs-independent-contractor-differences-you-need-to-know>`_
    This article explores the legal responsibilities and business partnership a contractor would have in comparison to an employee.

.. _finding_freelance_and_remote:

----------------------------------
Finding freelance and remote work
----------------------------------

`Truelancer <http://www.truelancer.com>`_
    Truelancer is a Global Community of Trusted Freelancers. Post your projects, find work, and get hired. 

`Top Coder <http://www.topcoder.com/>`_
    This is a competition website that, at its core, is a place to find freelance work. 

`Freelanship <https://freelanship.com/>`_
    Another great place to find freelance work.
    
`Free Code Camp <http://freecodecamp.com/>`_
    This open source community gives people opportunities to learn to code and then give back by helping non-profit organizations.

`Upwork <https://www.upwork.com/>`_
    Upwork is an awesome tool for finding freelance jobs. International students in particular may find this helpful because Upwork handles payment through its own system. 

`Remote OK <https://remoteok.io/>`_
    While this resource is not technically about freelance, this is a great resource for finding remote work.

`We Work Remotely <https://weworkremotely.com/>`_ 
    This is a great place to find and list jobs that aren’t restricted by commutes or a particular geographic.

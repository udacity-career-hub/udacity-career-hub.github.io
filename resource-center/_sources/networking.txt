.. _networking:

**********
Networking
**********

Building relevant professional relationships – or networking – can make a huge difference during the job process.

.. _informational_interviews:

========================
Informational Interviews
========================

A great way to build your network is through informational interviews, an informal meeting with someone from the industry you're interested in.

`The Muse: How to Ask for an Informational Interview <https://www.themuse.com/advice/how-to-ask-for-an-informational-interview-and-get-a-yes>`_
    How to get an informational interview.

`Interviewing Email Template <https://www.wellesley.edu/sites/default/files/assets/departments/cws/files/networking_emails.pdf>`_
    Stuck on how to write an email for various interviewing needs? Check out this template.

Check out the Career Development Workshop on `Informational Interviews <https://docs.google.com/document/d/1nYTZOUZc1jSOaRp2lFJgFM0hAlRWN7nU7lTpHxWkVnU/pub?embedded=true>`_! The video to the workshop can be found here: `Information Interviews Video <https://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&v=QI31xMQ8gqw>`_.

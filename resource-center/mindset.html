One of the most important aspects of becoming a programmer is learning how to think like one. This article defines the five elements of a developer mindset and why it is essential to your success in the field.

Written by a programmer with a non-traditional background, this article gives advice on how to prepare for and ace a programming interview. It details everything from the ABC (Always Be Coding) Mindset to exuding your passion in the interview.

Written by a programmer from an alternative background, this article details the story of his non-traditional career path. Within, he discusses four important steps to getting a rockstar job without a college degree.

Check out this list of recommended books to get you into the developer mindset - these texts will help you better understand the industry and how it relates to how people think.

There is an important difference between computer science and programming. You don’t just want to learn how to program, but you also want to learn how to think like a developer. Read this article to learn more! The article also features a TED Talk and a list of places to find relevant courses.

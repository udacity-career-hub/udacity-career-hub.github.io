Be in Demand

Udacity offers a comprehensive set of career development services that will help you build your resume, practice technical interviews, and connect with employers and work opportunities.

 

As part of your Nanodegree program, you can...

  • Build Your Careers Profile: Receive one-on-one feedback on your resume, LinkedIn, GitHub, and cover letter. You can access these career projects in your Nanodegree path!

  • Prep for Interviews: Practice job-specific interview questions and receive helpful critique and guidance. You can complete these dry-run interview courses and projects when you move closer to graduation in the following Nanodegree programs: Front-End Web Developer, Data Analyst, Full Stack Web Developer, iOS Developer, and Android Developer. Stay tuned for interview projects in other Nanodegree programs!

  • Mock Interviews: For further interview prep, complete Udacity's course on Technical Interview: Algorithms and Data Structures in Python in order to receive unlimited mock interview sessions on Pramp!

  • Get Job Opportunities: Get introduced to employers and recruiters through Udacity Talent Source. To take part in this program, make sure you complete your Udacity Professional Profile to best show off your talents to Udacity partner employers. When you're ready to turn on your Profile's "recruiter access", make sure to get a Udacity Professional Profile Review, a career project located toward the end of your Nanodegree path.

  • Receive Personalized Support at Each Step: Get one-on-one mentoring through 1:1 career appointments, community forums, and email with coaches and the Careers team.

  • Take Charge of Your Professional Development: Use Udacity's Career Resource Center to learn about industry standards; explore opportunities to develop your skills, such as open source projects or hackathons; and figure out your individual path to your dream job. Use what Udacity offers and pursue every opportunity you get!

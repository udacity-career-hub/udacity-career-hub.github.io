Be in Demand

Udacity offers a comprehensive set of career development services that will help you build your resume, practice technical interviews, and connect with employers and work opportunities.

 

As part of your Nanodegree program, you can...

  • Build Your Careers Profile: Receive one-on-one feedback on your resume, LinkedIn, and GitHub profiles. You can access these career projects as you pass projects in your Nanodegree path; they are also available in the Career Advisor: Front-End Web Developer program.

  • Prep for Interviews: practice job-specific interview questions and receive helpful critique and guidance. You can complete these dry-run interview courses and projects when you move closer to graduation in the following Nanodegree programs: Front-End Web Developer, Data Analyst, and Full Stack Web Developer; and Career Advisor: Front-End Web Developer program. Stay tuned for interview projects in other Nanodegree programs!

  • Get Job Opportunities: Get introduced to employers and recruiters through Udacity Talent Source. To take part in this program, make sure you complete your Udacity Profile to best show off your talents to Udacity partner employers. When you graduate from a Nanodegree program, you are also eligible to apply for The Udacity Experience, where you can gain a 2-month paid contract at Udacity!

  • Receive Personalized Support at Each Step: Get one-on-one mentoring through career appointments (you'll receive an email near graduation with directions to set up a meeting), community forums, and email with coaches and the Careers team.

  • Take Charge of Your Professional Development: Use Udacity's Career Resource Center to learn about industry standards; explore opportunities to develop your skills, such as through open source projects or hackathons; and figure out your individual path to your dream job. Use what Udacity offers and pursue every opportunity you get!
